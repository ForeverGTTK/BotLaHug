About BotLaHug

Your go-to solution for streamlined club and athlete management.

What We Offer

Welcome to BotLaHug, a comprehensive club management platform designed to make the administration of sports clubs, after-school programs, and athlete registrations seamless. Whether you're managing a local sports team or a larger organization, our platform provides the tools you need to succeed.

Key Features

  • Club Management: A centralized system for managing all club details, athletes, and events.
  • Athlete Registration: Register athletes for new seasons and events, track their progress, and store personal data securely.
  • Season & Class Scheduling: Easily organize and customize class schedules, keeping your members informed at all times.
  • Progress Tracking: Generate reports to track athlete development and monitor their progress over time.
  • Payment System: Manage payments with ease using our basic integrated system, with future support for online payments.

Why Choose BotLaHug?

At BotLaHug, we're not just about simplifying club management, we're about fostering growth. Whether you're a small organization looking to scale or a large entity needing advanced features, our platform adapts to your needs. With customizable club pages, full branding control, and powerful management tools, BotLaHug is your partner in success.

Looking Ahead

Here are some of the exciting future developments in store for BotLaHug:

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Added security for all user accounts.
  • Athlete Progress Tracking: Advanced visualization tools to monitor athlete growth and performance.
  • Automated Notifications: Send reminders and alerts for classes, payments, or upcoming events.
